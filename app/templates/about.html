About Songwall

Your Music, Your Ratings, Your Community

Songwall is a platform designed for music enthusiasts who love to discover, rate, and share music. Our mission is to create a space where your musical taste can be expressed, documented, and shared with others who share your passion.

What Can You Do on Songwall?

Discover Music

Browse through popular songs across different genres, see what's trending, and find new music based on what others with similar taste are enjoying. Our integration with Spotify makes it easy to listen to songs directly from our platform.

Rate Songs

Rate songs on a scale of 1-10 and add your personal comments about what makes a song special to you. Your ratings help create your unique musical profile and contribute to our community's discovery system.

Create Your Profile

Build your music profile with your ratings and reviews. Pin your current favorite song to showcase your current vibe. Customize your profile with a personal bio and theme color to express your personality.

Share and Connect

Post about songs you're enjoying, follow friends to see their ratings, and share your profile with others. Songwall makes it easy to connect with friends and discover music through people whose taste you trust.

How Songwall Works

Songwall connects with Spotify's catalog to provide you with access to millions of songs. When you search for and rate a song, we store your ratings in your profile. Your public profile showcases your top-rated songs and allows you to post thoughts about the music you love.

Our recommendation system analyzes your ratings to suggest similar songs and artists you might enjoy. As you rate more songs, our suggestions become increasingly personalized to your taste.
