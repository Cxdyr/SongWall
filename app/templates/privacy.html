Privacy Policy

Introduction

Last Updated: March 1, 2025

At Songwall, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have regarding your data on our platform.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information provided by you and song viewing information. This includes:

Information You Provide

  • Account information (email address, username, password)
  • Profile information (first name, bio, theme color preferences)
  • Content you create (ratings, reviews, posts, comments)
  • User relationships (follows, connections)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Song viewing and interaction data
  • Usage statistics and patterns
  • Device and browser information

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To personalize your experience and offer song recommendations
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our platform
  • To facilitate communication between users
  • To respond to your requests and inquiries

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Your information may be shared in the following circumstances:

  • With other users, as part of your public profile and activity on the platform
  • With service providers who assist in operating our platform
  • If required by law or to protect our rights

Your Choices and Rights

You have several rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and update your account information through your profile settings
  • Control the visibility of your ratings and posts
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to this Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Agreement

By using Songwall, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. Your continued use of our service following any changes to this policy constitutes your acceptance of those changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at songwallorg@gmail.com.
